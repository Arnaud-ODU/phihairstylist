An Experienced and Passionate Professional
Hi there!

I'm a dedicated hair stylist and colorist with 25 years of professional experience under my belt.

My journey in this industry has been enriched with TIGI certification, a testament to my commitment to developing and refining my skills.

My true passion lies in listening to people's stories and desires, enabling me to discover the perfect colors and styles that resonate with their personalities.

Over the years, I've gained extensive experience in wedding hair and makeup, helping brides look their absolute best on their special day.

Recently, I made a significant move from Virginia to Texas, embracing new challenges and opportunities in a fresh environment.

In my salon, I foster a relaxed and zen atmosphere, ensuring that every client's experience is as soothing and enjoyable as the results are beautiful.

Outside work, I cherish the simplicity and peace of long walks and meditation, practices that keep me grounded and inspired both personally and professionally.
